Olympic Pictograms

2021

While the 2020 Tokyo Olympics is delayed amid Covid-19, I had an opportunity to design Olympic pictograms. I chose to create five winter sports, ski jumping, speed skating, figure skating, snowboarding, and skeleton. I described muscle details of athletes and made them fit into the piece of the star background.
