Venice Tourism Logo

2021

Venice is a floating city in Italy, which has been designated as a UNESCO World Heritage Site. I was enamored by this city when I watched one music video filmed in Venice. For the logo, I used the shape of Ducale Palace's window as a border and placed Rialto's bridge inside it.
